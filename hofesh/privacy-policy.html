1. Overview

The Hofesh! app is a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by avih6 at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page informs visitors regarding policies with the handling, use, and disclosure of information for anyone deciding to use the Service.

By using the Service, there is an agreement to the policies described herein. Any information processed is used solely for providing and improving the Service. No information will be used or shared with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

The App does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Third-Party Analytics and Performance

The App uses third-party services that may process anonymous information for analytics and stability purposes:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: To understand App usage.
  • Google Analytics: To measure anonymous traffic data on this website.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To receive technical reports in case of an App crash.
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix performance-related issues.

External API (Hebcal)

The App communicates with Hebcal.com to retrieve holiday data. This communication is processed ephemerally to provide real-time updates. This process does not involve the storage of personal data or the tracking of user identity.

3. Log Data

In the event of an error, technical information is processed through third-party products called Log Data. This data is used solely for debugging purposes and includes the device hardware model, operating system version, and the time of the event.

4. Children’s Privacy

Families Policy Commitment

Hofesh! is compliant with the Google Play Families Policy.

Age-Appropriate Data Handling

There is no intentional collection of personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.
