Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Percentage Calculator (the "App"), developed by avih6, is provided as a utility for performing various percentage-based calculations. The App is designed with a privacy-first approach, ensuring that functionality is provided without the requirement for personal data collection or external synchronization.

1. Overview

This policy outlines how we handle information within the application. Our core principle is simple: your data stays on your device.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Personal Information

The App does not collect, harvest, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). Users are not required to create an account or provide any contact details to access the App's features.

Third-Party Services

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store and utilizes standard Google Play Services. These services may process certain non-identifying technical data for the following purposes:

  • Diagnostics: Monitoring application stability and identifying software crashes.
  • Performance: Gathering aggregated usage statistics to improve the Android platform.

All such data is managed by Google according to the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Processing

All calculations, inputs, and results are processed exclusively on the user's device. No calculation data is transmitted to external servers or third-party entities.

Calculation History and Settings

The App includes a "History" feature and data persistence options. All history records and user preferences are stored strictly within a private local database on the device.

  • This information is not synchronized with any cloud service.
  • Users may clear the stored history and preferences at any time through the App settings or by clearing the App's storage via the Android system settings.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from any users, including children under the age of 13.
