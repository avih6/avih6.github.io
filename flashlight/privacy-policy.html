1. Overview

avih6 built the Flashlight app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page informs visitors regarding policies with the handling, use, and disclosure of information for anyone deciding to use the Service.

By using the Service, there is an agreement to the policies described herein. The App is designed with a privacy-first approach; all hardware interactions and user configurations are handled locally on the device.

2. Data Handling & Permissions

The application requires access to specific device capabilities to function:

Camera & Flashlight Access

The App requests permission to access the camera hardware (`android.permission.CAMERA`). This is strictly required to control the camera flash and turn it on or off as a flashlight. We do not access, stream, record, or store any camera video, images, or audio feed.

Sensor Data (Orientation, Accelerometer)

The App processes real-time device orientation and rotation vector sensor data to render the UI compass and bubble level indicators, and processes accelerometer data to implement the shake-to-light feature. All sensor data is processed instantly in memory on the device and is never saved, cached, or transmitted to any servers.

Local Settings & Storage

Preferences (such as morse speed, auto-off timer configs, theme selection, pocket protection, and haptic options) are stored strictly locally on the user's device using Jetpack DataStore. No configuration data is synchronized to external clouds or servers.

3. Data Collection & Services

To ensure technical stability, optimize performance, and display advertisements, the App utilizes third-party providers that may process anonymous or pseudonymous information:

Third-Party Service Providers

  • Unity Ads: Used to display advertisements and mediation.
  • Google Play In-App Review: Used to request user reviews natively.
