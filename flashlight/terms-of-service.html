2. Purpose of the App

The App is a mobile utility tool designed to provide flashlight functionalities including torch light, strobe pulsing, SOS mode, screen illumination, disco light effects, and morse code transmitter utilities. It also features basic integrated sensors (Compass and Bubble Level) for convenience.

Important Safety Warning: The strobe and disco lighting features may cause discomfort or trigger seizures for individuals with photosensitive epilepsy. Use these modes with caution and avoid pointing the light directly at eyes.
