1. Overview

avhi6 built the New Driver app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page informs visitors regarding policies with the handling, use, and disclosure of information for anyone deciding to use the Service.

By using the Service, there is an agreement to the policies described herein. The App is designed with a privacy-first approach; all core driving data and profile configurations are handled locally on the device.

2. Local Data Handling

The following operations are performed locally on the user's device and do not involve data collection by the developer:

Local Personal Data

The application processes specific dates (such as birth date and driving test date) and allows users to log driving hours. This data is stored strictly locally in a private database on the user's device. No personal driving information is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers by the developer.

Device Synchronization

Data stored locally may be synchronized with the user’s own Wear OS devices or displayed via Android Auto and Android Automotive interfaces. This synchronization is handled locally or via standard system APIs and does not involve external data collection.

Data Deletion and Management

Users maintain full control over their data. The application provides a "Reset" function within the settings that permanently deletes all locally stored driving logs and configuration data from the device.

3. Data Collection and Usage

To ensure technical stability and improve the Service, the App utilizes third-party providers that may process anonymous information:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: To understand how the app is used.
  • Google Analytics: Used on this website to measure anonymous traffic data (such as page views) to help improve the service.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Performance Monitoring: Used to collect technical logs, stack traces, and performance metrics to identify and fix technical issues.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may process device identifiers and IP addresses to provide and personalize ads. Advertising is disabled during Android Auto/Automotive sessions. The display of ads is managed dynamically and may not be active at all times.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Used to deliver system updates and remote notifications.
