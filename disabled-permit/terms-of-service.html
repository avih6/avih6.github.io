2. Purpose of the App & Data Source

The App is a free utility tool designed to help users check the status of disabled parking permits for vehicles in Israel. It functions solely as a viewer and search interface.

All data provided through the App is retrieved in real-time from the official Open Data portal of the Israeli Government (data.gov.il). We do not create, modify, or host the permit database.
