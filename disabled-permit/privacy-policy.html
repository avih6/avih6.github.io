1. Overview

avih6 built the Disabled Permit Check app as a Free, ad-supported app. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page informs visitors regarding policies with the handling, use, and disclosure of information for anyone deciding to use the Service.

By using the Service, there is an agreement to the policies described herein. The App is designed with a privacy-first approach; search history and permit caching are handled locally on the device, and searches are performed without requiring user registration.

2. Device Permissions

To provide core features, the App requests specific device permissions. These hardware features are used strictly locally:

  • Camera Permission: Used exclusively for Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to scan vehicle license plates via Google ML Kit. Image processing occurs in real-time on your device. We do not record, save, or transmit photos or video to external servers.
  • Microphone Permission: Used exclusively for voice-to-text input to dictate license plate numbers. The audio processing is handled by standard Android system APIs. We do not record, save, or listen to your voice data.

3. Local Data Handling

The following operations are performed locally on the user's device and do not involve data collection by the developer:

Your search history and cached permit data (vehicles you have previously checked) are stored strictly locally in a private database on your device. No personal search history is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers by the developer. You maintain full control over your data and can delete individual history items or clear your entire search history directly from the App's interface.

4. Data Collection and Usage

To ensure technical stability, retrieve necessary data, and improve the Service, the App utilizes third-party providers and APIs:

data.gov.il API (Israeli Government)

When you search for a license plate, the App securely queries the official Israeli Government portal (data.gov.il). We only transmit the specific license plate number requested to retrieve the public permit status. As with any standard internet communication, your device's IP address may be visible to the government servers during these requests. However, the App does not associate the license plate number with you or your identity. The developer is not responsible for the accuracy or data retention policies of the government portal.

Technical & Ad Providers

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: To understand how the app is used (e.g., button clicks and screen views) using anonymous identifiers.
  • Google Analytics: Used on this website to measure anonymous traffic data (such as page views) to help improve the service.
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring & Crashlytics: Used to collect technical logs, stack traces, and performance metrics (like OCR processing times) to identify and fix technical issues.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may process device identifiers and IP addresses to provide and personalize ads.
