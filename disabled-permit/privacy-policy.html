1. Overview

The "Disabled Permit Check" application (the "App") is provided by avih6 as a Free app. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page informs users regarding the handling, use, and disclosure of information for anyone deciding to use the service.

By using the App, there is an agreement to the policies described herein. The information collected is used solely for providing and improving the service and will not be shared with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Handling

Personal Data

The App does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. All vehicle searches are processed ephemerally to provide real-time results.

Third-Party Analytics and Performance

To ensure technical stability and improve the service, the App utilizes third-party providers that may process anonymous information:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: To understand how the app is used.
  • Google Analytics: Used on this website to measure anonymous traffic data.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To receive technical reports in case of an app crash.
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix performance-related issues.

3. Government Data Source

The App retrieves data directly from the official Israeli Government Open Data portal (data.gov.il). This process does involve the developer storing, modifying, or owning the permit database.

4. Children’s Privacy

There is no intentional collection of personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. The App is designed as a utility tool for the general public.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Users are advised to review this page for any changes. Updates are effective immediately upon being posted on this page.
